Beef Soup

This hearty beef stew is a comforting and flavorful dish that's perfect for a cold day.
The tender beef, hearty vegetables, and rich broth combine to create a satisfying
and satisfying meal. The addition of Worcestershire sauce, thyme, and oregano
adds depth and complexity to the flavor profile, while the potatoes and frozen
vegetables provide a hearty and nutritious base.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 1 potato, cubed
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables

Instructions

  1. Brown the beef:
    • Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
    • Brown the beef on all sides.
    • Remove from the pot and set aside.
  2. Sauté vegetables:
    • In the same pot, sauté the onion, carrots, celery, and garlic until softened.
  3. Add broth and seasonings:
    • Stir in the Worcestershire sauce, thyme, oregano, and bay leaf.
    • Pour in the beef broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Simmer:
    • Reduce heat to low and simmer for 1-2 hours, or until the beef is tender.
  5. Add vegetables:
    • Stir in the potato and frozen vegetables.
    • Simmer for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Serve:
    • Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley.
