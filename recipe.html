Chicken Parmesan

This lightened-up version of classic chicken parmesan is
packed with protein, low in carbs, and bursting with flavour.
The breaded chicken cutlets are pan-fried to a golden crisp,
topped with a tangy tomato sauce, and melted mozzarella
cheese. It's a delicious and satisfying meal that won't leave
you feeling guilty.
